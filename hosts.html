GoDaddy

GoDaddy offers use of its data centers in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific for easy and quick access to your websites and databases from almost anywhere. All plans also come with automatic daily backup of your account. GoDaddy claims their servers have an up to 40% better response time than their sompetitors. GoDaddy protects their customers with 24/7 security that monitors for suspicious activities and any potential attacks.

GoDaddy also offers an add on called the Hub that allows users to manage multiple client sites in one place. The Hub can also send out client invoices in as little as 60 seconds as well as receive payments. With the Hub backups are kept in offsite starge and can be done hourly rather than daily. The Hub can also track website performance, provide analytics and draft reports.

GoDaddy offers several different plans varying in price, all of which are on a 3 year term. Although the prices are listed by what it would cost monthly, payment can only be made in full.

GoDaddy offers the best services, of the three companies listed above. It has the most options available with plenty of features at the most reasonable prices. GoDaddy also seems to be the most user friendly especially for the first time user, while bluehost and Host Gator seem to be more geared toward someone who is already more established and knows what they are doing rather that being beginner friendly. GoDaddy is easier to navigate and overall seems like a better program and company to work with.
